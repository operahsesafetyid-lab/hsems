# HOW TO BUILD YOUR .EXE — Step by Step

## What is GitHub?
GitHub is a FREE website that will build your .exe for you
in the cloud. You don't need Node.js or any commands.

---

## STEP 1 — Create Free GitHub Account
1. Go to: https://github.com
2. Click "Sign up"
3. Enter email, password, username
4. Verify your email
5. Done — account created

---

## STEP 2 — Create New Repository
1. Click the "+" icon at top right
2. Click "New repository"
3. Repository name: hse-management-system
4. Select "Private" (keeps your code private)
5. Click "Create repository"

---

## STEP 3 — Upload Your Files
1. On the repository page, click "uploading an existing file"
2. Drag and drop ALL files from the hse-final folder
   (including .github folder — very important!)
3. Click "Commit changes"

---

## STEP 4 — Watch It Build
1. Click the "Actions" tab at the top
2. You will see "Build HSE Management System" running
3. Wait 5-10 minutes
4. When it shows a GREEN tick — it is done!

---

## STEP 5 — Download Your .EXE
1. Click on the completed build (green tick)
2. Scroll down to "Artifacts"
3. Click "HSE-Management-System-Setup"
4. A ZIP downloads — open it
5. Inside is your .exe installer!

---

## STEP 6 — Install on Any PC
1. Copy the .exe to any Windows PC
2. Double-click it
3. Click Install
4. Done! App appears in Start Menu

---

## IMPORTANT NOTES
- The .exe works on ANY Windows 10/11 PC
- No internet needed after installation
- Data saves permanently on each PC
- You can share the .exe with anyone
